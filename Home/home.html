About the Initiative

Rooted in Purpose, Powered by Watts Group

Watts Community is the CSR and Education arm of Watts Group Ltd. — an AI-first Canadian consulting and investment group with roots since 2008 and incorporation in 2021.

We believe that a strong community is the foundation of lasting success. That's why we've committed resources, expertise, and our professional network to build a space where growth isn't gatekept — it's shared.
